> **Site migration in progress.** I'm in the middle of moving my posts over from
> Medium to GitHub Pages, so expect this site to grow as content lands here.
{: .prompt-info }

This is the new home for my security research and writing — consolidated from
scattered posts into one place I actually control.

## What this site is for

- **Vulnerability research** — root-cause writeups, CVE analysis, exploit
  development notes.
- **Offensive security** — technique deep-dives and (sanitized) tradecraft.
- **Tooling** — small things I build, with links to the code.
- **Notes** — shorter posts and paper roundups.

Posts are written in plain Markdown and the source lives on
[GitHub](https://github.com/huskersec/huskersec.github.io). If you spot an
error, an issue or PR is welcome.

## Why GitHub Pages

Markdown in, version control, full control over presentation, and good code
formatting — which matters when half your post is a disassembly listing.
